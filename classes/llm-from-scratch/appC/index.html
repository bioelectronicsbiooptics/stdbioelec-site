Chapter 5

Exercise 5.1

"pizza" sampled 0x at temperature 0 or 0.1; sampled 32x at temperature 5. Estimated probability: 3.2%. Actual: 4.3%.

Exercise 5.2

  • Low top-k (<10) + temperature <1: deterministic, coherent (formal documents, code)
  • High top-k (20-40) + temperature >1: creative, diverse (brainstorming, fiction)

Exercise 5.3

Two ways to force deterministic behavior: (1) top_k=None with no temperature scaling; (2) top_k=1.

Chapter 7

Exercise 7.1

Phi-3 prompt format with modified format_input. Fine-tuning ~17% faster; score ~50.

Exercise 7.2

Mask instructions in targets: set targets[:instruction_length-1] = -100. Performance slightly worse (~4 points), consistent with the paper.

Exercise 7.3

Fine-tune on Stanford Alpaca (52,000 entries -- 50x more data). GPU recommended.

Exercise 7.4

Instruction fine-tune with LoRA (rank=16, alpha=16). On Nvidia L4: ~28% faster (1.30 min vs 1.80 min). Score ~50.
